An Yujin

IZ*ONE member

An Yujin (안유진) is currently an idol under Off The Record Entertainment and Starship Entertainment. She was a competitor on Produce 48.
She ranked #5 on the finale, making her a member of IZ*ONE.

Career & History

On October 29, 2018, Yujin made her official debut with IZ*ONE.
